Welcome to Identi-Find!

Hello! Welcome to Indenti-Find! Have you asked yourself the questions "Who am I?" or "What is my purpose?" before? Well, then this website is what you're looking for! In this website, I'll give you a proper tour on what Identity really is. Problems that are closely related to Identity such as Identity theft and Fabrication will also be mentioned later on in this website as well as Identity Crisis.

The whole aim of this website is to spread awareness on Identity crisis and theft and how problematic, or even life-threathening, it is to normal citizens like us. In the following pages, there lie more information about Identity in a general scale, How the Identity crisis and theft started, Further information about Identity theft, Fabrication and Identity Crisis, and how people are handling it in recent times.




Short Fact About Identity:

Identity Theft actually happens in a regular basis all around the World! A lot of the cases that involve identity theft are related to tax frauds too!




Fun Fact About Identity:

Did you know that your Identity is the sole drive for your actions? This is because we view things differently depending on who we think we are.
